Immortal Aquarium

Touch prototype — three tankmates, one per tab. Best held in one hand, poked with the other.

Pet his head with a finger · pull his body to stretch him

Double-tap to puff · pinch or press-and-hold to squeeze her

Hold both glowing ends with two fingers and circle your fingers on the dots to spin · pull the dots apart to focus the whirl in her middle

Gestures from the storyboard: Bubbles sheds bubbles when petted or pulled; Poppy puffs like a balloon and does the Panic Pete eye-pop; Slink is a centripetal spinner. Characters pause when their tab is hidden.
